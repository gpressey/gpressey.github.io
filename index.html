Why it matters

🚲

Protected bike lanes reduce serious injuries for everyone on the road — not just cyclists.

🚶

Wider sidewalks make Marlee accessible for strollers, wheelchairs, and everyone in between.

🏘️

Safer streets help local businesses thrive — people on foot and bikes stop more often.

Write to our Councillor

Your voice matters. Send a quick note to Councillor Colle and Mayor Chow asking them to prioritize safe streets on Marlee.

Dear Councillor Colle and Mayor Chow, I am a resident of the Marlee Village neighbourhood and I am writing to ask you to prioritize safe streets on Marlee Avenue. Our neighbourhood needs protected bike lanes and wider sidewalks so that children, seniors, and families can get around safely. As density increases along Marlee, the need for safe, accessible streets only grows. Please move forward with the Beltline Gap Connection and the Eglinton TOday improvements without further delay. Our community is counting on you. Thank you for your time and consideration. [Your name] [Your address, optional]
